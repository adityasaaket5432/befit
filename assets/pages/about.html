About Us

We offer a multi-faceted approach to changing the way Consumers/employees approach their health that includes developing a healthy workplace culture, forming trusted health advisor/ employee relationships and providing quality and cost-effective local care options is key to establishing long-term, sustainable reductions in medical costs.

Our Story

KeepMeFit, a technology company devoted to helping people achieve their health and wellness goals, has implemented and tested innovative consumer engagement and activation strategies and worksite cultural changes that directly reflect the mission to help people live their lives to the fullest. KeepMeFit acts as a personal advocate to help them navigate a complex health care system and local market expertise that empowers them to access the best care with quality and cost in mind.
